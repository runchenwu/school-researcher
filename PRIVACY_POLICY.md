# Privacy Policy

**Last Updated: January 4, 2026**

## Introduction

School Researcher ("we," "our," or "the App") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

## Information We Collect

### Information You Provide

- **API Keys**: You may provide API keys for AI services (OpenAI, Google Gemini, or Anthropic). These keys are stored locally on your device and are never transmitted to our servers.
- **Search Queries**: Your college research queries are sent directly to the AI provider you configure (OpenAI, Google, or Anthropic) to generate responses.
- **Favorites**: Schools you save to your favorites list are stored locally on your device.

### Information We Do NOT Collect

- We do **not** collect personal identification information
- We do **not** track your location
- We do **not** access your contacts, photos, or other device data
- We do **not** use analytics or tracking services
- We do **not** have user accounts or registration

## Data Storage

All data is stored **locally on your device** using secure storage mechanisms:

- API keys are stored in encrypted local storage
- Search history and favorites are stored in local app storage
- No data is transmitted to or stored on our servers

## Third-Party Services

The App integrates with the following third-party AI services based on your configuration:

### OpenAI
- When you use OpenAI as your AI provider, your queries are sent to OpenAI's API
- OpenAI's privacy policy: [https://openai.com/privacy](https://openai.com/privacy)

### Google Gemini
- When you use Gemini as your AI provider, your queries are sent to Google's API
- Google's privacy policy: [https://policies.google.com/privacy](https://policies.google.com/privacy)

### Anthropic (Claude)
- When you use Anthropic as your AI provider, your queries are sent to Anthropic's API
- Anthropic's privacy policy: [https://www.anthropic.com/privacy](https://www.anthropic.com/privacy)

**Note**: Your use of these third-party services is subject to their respective privacy policies. We recommend reviewing their policies before using their services.

## Data Sharing

We do **not** sell, trade, or share your information with third parties. Your data remains on your device and is only transmitted to the AI provider you explicitly configure.

## Data Security

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your information:

- API keys are stored using secure, encrypted storage
- All API communications use HTTPS encryption
- No sensitive data is logged or transmitted to external servers

## Children's Privacy

The App is designed for high school students researching colleges. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. The App does not require account creation or collection of personal data.

## Your Rights

You have the right to:

- **Delete your data**: Clear all app data through your device settings or within the app
- **Export your data**: Export your favorites list from within the app
- **Control your data**: All data is stored locally and under your control

## Data Retention

- Data is retained locally on your device until you delete it
- Uninstalling the app removes all locally stored data
- We do not retain any data on external servers

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by updating the "Last Updated" date at the top of this policy. We encourage you to review this Privacy Policy periodically.
